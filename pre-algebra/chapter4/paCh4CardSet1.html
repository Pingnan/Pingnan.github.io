Lisa ran a lemonade stand last Saturday. She agreed to pay her parents 30% of the profit. She made a profit of $25.
How much money did Lisa pay her parents?
`30% xx 25 = 0.3 xx 25 = 7.5`
Laura's class has `32` students. There are `20` girls in her class.
What is the percentage of girls?
`20/32 = 0.625 = 62.5%`
Sarah has a big jar of change in her room. She has 300 coins in total, and 120 of them are pennies.
What percentage of the coins are pennies?

`120/300 = 0.4 = 40%`

Bridget is shopping with her mom and wonders how much shopping is left to do. "We already have 60% of the items on our list," her mom says. Bridget sees 12 items in the cart.
How many items are on the list?

`12/(60%) = 12/0.6 = 20`

A zoo has 27 Emperor penguins which make up 30% of all the penguins at the zoo.
How many penguins live at the zoom?

`27 / (30%) = 27/0.3 = 90`

In the United States, 13 out of every 20 cans are recycled.
What percent of cans are recycled?
`13/20 = 0.65 = 65%`
