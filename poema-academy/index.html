Poema Academy

Poema academy provides a brand new kind of education which is designed for the new economy and global marketplace. It is a two year high school problem in which students are trained to become global entrepreneurs. Graduates have the options of going to a college prep program, a trade school, or start his or her business with the support of the school.

Our curriculum consists of five subjects:

  • Biblical Entrepreneurship
  • Practical Math
  • Practical English
  • Business Information Technology
  • Business Operation

Students will spend half day learning in school and half day working in different businesses with the objective of learning how to run a global business. They are required to submit weekly reports for fulfilling the requirements for Business Operation .
