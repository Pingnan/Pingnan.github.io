Poema Academy

Poema academy provides a brand new kind of education which is designed for the new economy and global marketplace. It is a two year high school where students are trained to become entrepreneurs. Graduates have the options of going to a college prep program, a trade school, or start a business.

Our curriculum consists of five subjects:

  • Biblical Entrepreneurship
  • Practical Math
  • Practical English
  • Computer Technology
  • Business operation

Students will spend half day learning in school and half day work in different businesses with the objective of running a business. They are required to submit reports.
