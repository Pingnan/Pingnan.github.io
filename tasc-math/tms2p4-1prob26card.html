Problem

26. To make the top of a dining room table, Craig glues a piece of oak that is `5 / 16` inch thick to a piece of pine that is `7 / 8` inch thick. What is the total thickness, in inches, of the tabletop?

A. `9 / 16`
B. `1 3 / 16`
C. `1 1 / 4`
D. `1 9/16`

Solution

B. `1 3/16`

`5/16 + 7/8 = 5/16 + 14/16`

`= 19/16 = 1 3/16`
