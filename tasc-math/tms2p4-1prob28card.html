Problem

28. At a fabric store, Melissa sold `8 7/8` yards of cloth to a customer. If the material was cut from a bolt of fabric containing `23 1/4` yards, how many yards are left on the bolt?

A. `14 3/8`
B. `15 3/8`
C. `15 3/4`
D. `31 7/8`

Solution

C. `14 3/8`

`23 1/4 - 8 7/8 = \(23-8\) + \(1/4-7/8\)`

`=15 + \((1/4)*(2/2) - 7/8 \)`

`= 15 + \(2/8 - 7/8\) = 15 + (2-7)/8`

`= 15 + (-5/8) = 14 + 1 - 5/8`

`= 14 + (8-5)/8 = 14 + 3/8 = 14 3/8`
