Problem

27. Carol will use two bolts to assemble a book cart. Bolt A is `2 7/8` inches long, bolt B is `1 1/4` inches long. How much longer, in inches, is bolt A than bolt B?

A. `5/8`
B. `1 3/8`
C. `1 5/8`
D. `1 3/4`

Solution

C. `1 5/8`

`2 7/8 - 1 1/4 = \(2-1\) + \(7/8-1/4\)`

`=1+7/8 - (1*2)/(4*2) = 1 + 7/8 - 2/8`

`=1 + (7-2)/8 = 1 + 5/8 = 1 5/8`
